How to Buy?

  1. Log in to your TMetric account. If you don't have one, sign up now.
  2. On the main menu, click Workspace and select Subscription.
  3. On the subscription page click Upgrade Now.
  4. Enter your team size, select subscription plan, and click Proceed To Checkout.
  5. On the shopping cart page enter your Billing Information and select Payment Options.
  6. Click Continue to place an order.
